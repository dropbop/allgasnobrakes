Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in a photography session?

Each session includes consultation, professional photography, basic editing, and delivery of high-resolution images. Additional retouching and prints are available.

How long does a typical shoot take?

Sessions typically range from 2-4 hours depending on the scope, number of vehicles, and locations. We'll discuss timing during consultation.

Do you provide locations?

Yes! I have access to various stunning locations and can recommend the best settings based on your vehicle and desired aesthetic.

What's the turnaround time for images?

You'll receive a preview gallery within 48 hours and final high-resolution images within 1-2 weeks after the session.
